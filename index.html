Overview

King David Contracting LLC is a New Jersey-based minority-owned business providing end-to-end facilities support, including construction, landscaping, janitorial, and staffing services. We are registered in SAM.gov and eligible for federal contracts under a broad range of NAICS codes. We prioritize excellence, compliance, and reliability in every engagement.
