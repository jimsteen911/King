We Locate Unclaimed Funds That Belong to You

Legally sourced from public records. No Social Security Numbers. No risk. No upfront cost. Pay only if we recover funds — via escrow.

Our Founder's Story

James Steenland, the founder of The Asset Locator, rose from homelessness and addiction to build a service that helps others reclaim what’s rightfully theirs. Four months clean and armed with nothing but an iPhone, he taught himself to code, navigated complex public databases, and founded this company to ensure that people with the fewest resources can still access unclaimed funds. His journey from poverty and despair fuels our commitment to transparency, integrity, and compassion.

How It Works

  • We search dozens of public databases using your name and known addresses; only you have your unique address.
  • You never need to give us your Social Security Number — ever.
  • If we find a valid claim, we notify you and present the documentation.
  • We handle all paperwork with your written consent and limited representation agreement.
  • Recovered funds are deposited into escrow and disbursed: 80% to you, 20% to us.
  • If nothing is recovered, you owe nothing. Zero risk.

Safe, Transparent Transactions with Escrow.com

Once you’ve created an account with Escrow.com, making secure transactions using your bank account becomes quick, clean, and transparent. Real-time status updates ensure you always know exactly where your money is in the process.

Contrary to popular belief, digital transactions are often safer. Escrow.com’s fully documented system eliminates paper clutter and provides organized tracking from start to finish.

Need support? No problem. Backed by experienced escrow professionals, Escrow.com provides live phone assistance Monday to Friday (8:00 AM – 4:00 PM PST) and typically responds to emails within 12 hours.
