About Us

King David Contracting LLC is a federal contractor and public service business operated by James Steenland. The company is built from the ground up with a mission to recover value — in properties, people, and public records.

We’re proudly based in New Jersey and specialize in asset location, recovery assistance, construction labor, and disaster support. Whether it’s unclaimed property or underutilized potential, we help bring it home.
